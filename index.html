Benita's Story

Benita is an African-decent actress. She was born in Congo but has lived in Belgium for more than 15 years. She discovered acting at the age of 16. After watching "Requiem for a Dream" she decided she wanted to go for an acting career. She travels worldwide to Paris, Canada, New York and Los angeles to do what she loves. English, French and Dutch are the languages she masters perfectly. Scroll down to take a look at her resume.
